---
title: Project Clickingtown
description: Casual clicker written in Vue
---

Clickingtown is casual clicker game, currently for computers. For learning Vue I just wanted to create something else than yet another todo app. Data structures and game logic can be far more complex than simple list of tasks in todo app.

In game you are medieval viceroy, and player task is to expand and develop city. In comparison to typical clickers where they have one currency, here player have 5 resources all needed to build and upgrade buildings. Player starts with small amount of all resources, one building that provides one of resource, and after gathering required materials we can build another building.
